## ★ Top 5 to prototype next

1. **The Redline** *(Fair Housing as a live edit)* - before Dana sees any client draft, a Fair-Housing pass renders a **visible diff**: risky phrase struck in amber, safe rewrite underlaid in green, per-category lozenge (familial status, race, disability). Tap to override = accountable, logged. *"perfect for a young family, safe churches nearby" rewrites itself into feature-based language in real time, and she never had to know the rule.* `Load: strong · Visual: high · Agentic: med · M`
2. **The Mirror** *(supervise the client conversation)* - a **phone-inside-a-phone**: the client's actual thread in their bubble style, nested in Dana's chrome; Leo's next message sits as a **dashed bubble with a countdown-to-send ring** + an AiRM "reviewed, clear" tag. Tap to hold/edit/send-now; pull down to step in. *Supervised an AI conversation with one thumb, no typing.* `strong · high · high · M`
3. **Closing Day Cockpit** - the 24 hours that can detonate, as a **runway of status gates** (wire verified → walkthrough → clear-to-close → signed → funded → recorded), each green/amber/red; a persistent **wire-fraud interlock** on any wiring-change. *The silence that would surface at the closing table surfaces at 11am instead.* `strong · high · high · L`
4. **The Bridge** *(Leo originates, not just triages)* - reasons across Dana's whole client graph to propose a **human introduction** (a past buyer outgrowing a starter home as a fitting listing appears). Two portraits joined by a drawing arc with the why-now; a **double-opt-in consent ladder**; matching is behavioral, never protected-class. `some · high · high · M`
5. **Calibration Card** - from any confidence chip, Leo's **own track record per tier, misses included** ("high: right 94% across 211 calls; here are the 13 I got wrong"), with a drift line showing it self-correcting. *The word "high" becomes a measured promise.* `strong · med · high · M`

## The full set, grouped

### Responsible AI & trust (build as one interlocking spine)
- **The Redline** - see above.
- **Calibration Card** - see above.
- **The Counter** *(respectful disagreement)* - when Dana commands something the evidence argues against, Leo stages it but surfaces **one** Counter card with its reasoning, then yields gracefully; logs both, feeds the Calibration Card. *"Before I drop Maple to 580: three comps closed above 600 in 9 days, and you have a Saturday showing. Hold?"* `solid · med · high · S`
- **The Sightline** *(what Leo can and cannot see)* - a per-deal field-of-view: connected sources lit, **blind spots greyed with honest consequences** ("I can't see her texts to your personal cell, so if she went cold there I won't know"); a consent ledger to revoke a source and watch the view dim. `solid · high · med · M`

### Deal lifecycle (beyond the morning)
- **Closing Day Cockpit** - see above.
- **The Five-Minute Window** *(speed-to-lead)* - the instant a lead lands, Leo has enriched it and pre-built first contact; a full-screen takeover with a soft **draining arc**, the single highest-leverage fact, three one-tap responses, and "route to team" by territory + load. Auto-sends at minute 4 on a standing order. `strong · high · high · M`
- **Doorframe** *(open-house co-pilot)* - a glanceable **room rail**: each sign-in drops in as a chip with a warmth bar that fills as Leo enriches (behavior-only, never demographics); tap for a two-line whisper + a staged AiRM-reviewed follow-up. *Three follow-ups drafted before she's out of the driveway.* `strong · high · high · M`
- **The Wake** *(the night a deal dies)* - an overnight post-mortem as a calm, low-contrast card: **Cause · honest Yours/Not-yours split · Salvage** (the buyer who lost is still a buyer). One swipe banks the lesson into Leo's risk memory. No blame, no metrics. `solid · med · high · M`

### Collaboration & client-facing
- **The Mirror** - see above.
- **The Bridge** - see above.
- **Two-Key** *(the send that needs two humans)* - for irreversible actions (earnest-money release, price reduction, Fair-Housing-sensitive message), a **two-keyhole lock bar**: Dana press-and-holds her key; the co-agent/broker/compliance presses theirs on their device and the halves **seat together** on both screens. Either can attach a 5-second voice condition that blocks it. `solid · high · med · M`

### Multi-surface
- **Wheel Mode** *(voice-only, driving)* - a CarPlay-style surface for the 12-minute drives; Leo triages by voice, **loosens autonomy** for low-stakes ("running 10 late") but **hard-stops to read-only** for anything client-facing touching money or Fair Housing. *Catches the dangerous question and reframes it at 40 mph.* `strong · med · high · M`
- **Guest Mode** *(hand the phone to the client)* - one tap seals off everything internal (Silence Ledger, cap/commission, Leo's private notes, other agents) and shows a warm, Fair-Housing-safe client view in Dana's brand; a persistent border + Face-ID to exit. `strong · high · med · M`

### Human factors & character
- **The Tell** *(burnout, inferred)* - Leo never asks how she's doing; it **infers strain** from her own fingerprint (pre-6am first-touch for days, rising reply latency, climbing deferrals) and offers to **subtract work** ("you've started before 6 four mornings straight; want me to hold the team's non-urgent items till noon?"). Never repeats within a week. `strong · med · high · S`
- **The Close Bloom** *(celebration scaled to cost)* - when a deal funds, no generic confetti; Leo pulls the deal's history and renders a **"what this one took"** moment, one restrained light-bloom on the word "Closed," scaled to difficulty, then settles into the thank-you draft + the cap/REAX-equity counter ticking. `solid · high · high · M`

## Honest notes
- **The responsible-AI four interlock** - Calibration Card is *fed by* The Counter and by owned misses (The Wake). Build them as **one trust spine** sharing an event-log + outcomes store, or they fragment.
- Every client-facing idea holds the hard line: **behavior-only scoring, AiRM review before any send, human escalation on the unanswerable.** That line is the product's integrity.
- Cut for overlap with prior art: coach-cards (= The Bench), I-think-vs-I-know (= categorical confidence), signing-off (= Wind-Down), the-handoff (= escalate-to-human), various recap variants.
- **Strongest single bet:** **The Redline** - it turns an abstract legal constraint into a premium, panel-surprising interaction no competitor shows, and it's the clearest argument that *AI in real estate must be designed, not bolted on.*
