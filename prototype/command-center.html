How Leo speaks one voice, five states
Thinking
A calm breathing dot, never a blocking spinner. "Market-Watch is reading 14 comps…"
Uncertain · the hand-up
Surfaces its own doubt + two options. Never guesses on your behalf, judgment returns to the human.
Confident
"Done. Here's what changed." Always reversible, an 8-second Undo. Confidence is never arrogance.
Silent
The default for ~90% of work. Collapsed into "47 handled." Earns trust by not interrupting.
Wrong / corrected
Owns the miss, you correct it, it visibly learns and logs for audit. Caught a Fair-Housing risk before sending.

Every status dot in the app speaks in one of these five states. The guided tour shows each one where it lives.

The rule, in one line

An agent earns a pixel only when it is uncertain, the stakes are high, or it needs your voice. Everything else is silent (handled), watching, or routed to a teammate. Signal hierarchy is enforced by the orchestrator, not by you scrolling.

Accountability for the silence

The trust ledger at the top accounts for what was deliberately not shown: every silently-handled action carries a tappable provenance stamp (which policy authorized it), and any correction tightens that policy, not just a dismiss.
