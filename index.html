How I got here

The walkthrough, in four beats. Where I started, what I did, what changed, and the answer.

01
Where I started
I researched the team lead's real day. She lives at two altitudes that fight for the same hours: her own deals, and her team's. The dread underneath it all is a deal dying in silence. And the scarce resource is trust, already spent by tools that were confidently wrong. So the brief wrote itself: less, ranked, and honest about its own confidence.
02
What I did
I generated 30 concepts across two waves, then built 22 of them as live demos, because a triage product is timing and state, not a still picture. I tried the whole screen as three visual directions and twenty fused variants, and studied ten best-in-class app aesthetics to map the space before committing.
03
What changed
I started dark. A reviewer called dark "AI slop," so I detoured to warm, editorial light to earn calm by daylight. That detour was honest, but it gave up the atmosphere the product wanted. So I came back to a disciplined dark: a chosen emerald on near-black, with warm sand reserved for what needs a human. Then I adopted a stronger wireframe and re-skinned it to this exact system.
04
The answer
One calm, dark command center. The few things that need a human, ranked across your own deals and your team's. The reasoning shown, not hidden. And review before send, so nothing leaves in your name until you tap "Send as me." That is the prototype.

For the full chronology, the decisions, and the dead ends, read the build journal.

The lead, polished to highest craft

the lead artifact · interactive prototype
The morning command center
One self-contained file. The Today tab is the finished pitch: Leo's overnight trust ledger, one focus card at a time, your own deals and the team's risk ranked together, an Approve flow that runs live in the Dynamic Island, the voice outbox you review before it sends, and the Fair-Housing tripwire. Today is the heart of the pitch, and Day, Team, and Leads round out the surface: a timeline of agent-prepped stops, the team stacked and each teammate's pipeline a tap away, and ICP-scored lead routing you can reassign. Switch My deals and My team to change altitude, flip light or dark from the top right, or take the guided tour to walk the morning one beat at a time.
